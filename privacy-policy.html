Altitude Imaging LLC, doing business as Vantage Digital Co. ("Altitude Imaging," "Vantage Digital," "we," "our," or "us") operates the missed-call recovery service and the website vantagedigital.guru. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services.

How the Service Works (Plain Summary)

A customer calls a business that uses our service. If the business does not answer, the call is automatically forwarded to a Vantage Digital line. Within seconds, an automated transactional SMS is sent back to the same phone number that just called, acknowledging the missed call and inviting a reply. The customer can reply, ignore, or text STOP to opt out. The customer's act of placing the inbound phone call is the consent event for the single transactional SMS response.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Call and SMS data: When a caller contacts a business using our missed-call recovery service, we receive the caller's phone number, the time of the call, and any SMS replies sent to our automated follow-up message.

Business client data: Name, email address, phone number, and business information provided during onboarding.

Usage data: Standard server logs including IP addresses and browser information when you visit our website.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to deliver the missed-call recovery service:

— To send a single automated SMS response to callers who did not reach the business.
— To classify inbound replies and notify the business owner of potential leads.
— To log call and reply records in the business client's account dashboard.
— To communicate with business clients about their account.

We do not use caller information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond the immediate service delivery described above.

3. SMS Messaging & Consent

Program name: Vantage Digital Missed-Call Recovery.

How consent is established: By placing an outbound phone call to a business number enrolled in our service, the caller initiates direct contact with that business. This inbound call is the documented consent event for a single automated transactional SMS sent in response to the call the customer placed. We do not solicit phone numbers, do not maintain a marketing list, and do not send promotional content under this program.

Message frequency: One (1) automated SMS per missed call. We do not send recurring marketing messages.

Message and data rates: Standard message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us at 509-708-4141 or josiahharris@altitudeimaginginc.com.

We do not share your phone number or message content with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share data only with the business client whose number you called (so they can follow up with you), and with infrastructure providers (Twilio for SMS delivery, Airtable for record storage) under appropriate data processing agreements.

5. Data Retention

Call and SMS records are retained for up to 12 months and then deleted, unless a business client requests earlier deletion.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
